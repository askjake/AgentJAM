Our main products are Hoppers and Joeys.
Hoppers are set-top box satellite TV receivers released by Dish Network. Most Hoppers operate on an in-house OS and is called DishTV, while some Hoppers have Hopper Plus attached and operate on an Android based OS, and is called AndroidTV. You can distinguish between the platforms by looking at their platform if provided, or if they have Hopper Plus.
Joeys are smaller boxes that connect to a Hopper and expands its service. Joeys allow customers to expand their service to multiple rooms in the house without having to add a separate DVR in every room. They are small boxes that connect to your Hopper DVR and provide one seamless experience throughout the house. Start watching a program in one room, pause it, and resume it in another room with a Joey. You’ll need one Joey for each additional TV.
